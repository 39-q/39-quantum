# 39 Quantum

**Read. Publish. Explain.**

A platform for quantum research papers and community explanations.

[Live Site](https://three9-quantum.onrender.com)

---

## What It Is

39 Quantum is a place where anyone can:

- **Read** explanations of quantum research papers
- **Publish** their own research
- **Explain** papers to help others understand

It's built for students, enthusiasts, and new researchers who want to make quantum research more accessible.

---

## How It Works

1. A researcher publishes a paper (or links one from arXiv)
2. Anyone in the community writes an explanation
3. Others read, discuss, and learn
