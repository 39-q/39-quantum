# 39 Quantum

**Publish. Verify. Fund.** A decentralized platform for quantum research on Algorand.

[Live Demo](https://three9-quantum.onrender.com) | [X](https://x.com/39quantum)

---

## What This Is

39 Quantum is a free platform where quantum researchers can:

- **Upload** their thesis
- **Get an AI summary** (Llama 3.2)
- **Receive an immutable timestamp** (Algorand blockchain)
- **Connect with funders** (individual investors)

No fees. No tokens. No VCs. Just a tool for researchers.

---

## Why This Exists

Quantum research is hard to fund. Patents lock up technology. Researchers in developing countries have little access to capital. This platform aims to change that.

---

## How It Works

1. **User submits a thesis** (title, authors, abstract, funding URL)
2. **Backend calls Llama 3.2 AI** via OpenRouter to generate a simplified summary
3. **Thesis + summary are saved** to the backend
4. **User connect Metamask or another Base compatible wallet** to pay for only the Base chain gas fees
5. **Papers are displayed publicly** for readers and potential funders
