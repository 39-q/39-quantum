About 39

Quantum computing is a revolutionary technology. But, there is a huge gap between what researchers receive in developed countries with huge investments in quantum technology meanwhile researchers residing in poorer and less economically stronger countries have little to nothing in financing from the respective government organizations . This platform aims to record research papers into the blockchain and help quantum physicist's to connect with individual investors and quantum hobbyists around the world with built-in with a built-in AI model that summarizes research papers into more simpler terms.
